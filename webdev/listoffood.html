GREEN PARK ~ activities

We offer great entertainment for families and groups.

Available Activities

Rides and Games

This section of the park is to play, be amused and have fun with loved ones.
On this section, there are different types of roller costers, skating and a
whole lot of other games. There are also freak shows, acrobatics, conjuring,
juggling and alot more.

Food Spot

There are food, joints, all kinds of restaurants, pubs, fast food joints and alot more.

Hotel

There is the Green Royal hotel for people that would love to spend a vacation or holidays. The hotel come in classes and there is a pool provided for all clients and good food services.

Under 6

This section is for children under the age 6. In this sections there is a
setted up playng ground for the children. There is also a nursury where you
can register our kid and leave then there with the nuns for them to take care.

Horror and Adventures

There are more games in this section. These games are of adventures and are very scary.

Picnics and Get togethers

This section of the park is been divided into two. The first section is where all get togethers
can take place like BBQ, camping and other get togethers that includes alots of people.

Greenery

This section of the park is for picnics, small joints and for play.

Forest

We also have a friendly reserved forest. Forest is used for hiking, camping and others.

Festivals

Festival are held at the greenery park during summer. Huge artist are mostly invited to partake.

Rentals

Our gardens that comes with the Green lake can be rented for ceremonies
and also other greenery sections of our park.
We also have halls and restaurants that can be rented for ocassions and ceremonies.
